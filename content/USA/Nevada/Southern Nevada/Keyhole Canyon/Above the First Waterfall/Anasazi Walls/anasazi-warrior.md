# Description
Face climb past a bolt to a hand crack. Belay with gear, walk off descent.# Location
On the rightmost facet of the Anasazi cliffs, sticks out a little farther west than the other segments. It's the hand crack on the left side that doesn't reach the ground.# Protection
One bolt, gear to 3".
