# Description
Pull a small roof right off the ground on juggy, semi-manky rock to a ledge under a small dihedral on the arete 15' up (there's no pro before the dihedral). Climb the dihedral to a left angling hand crack and step right to the arete when it ends. Step left onto a bottoming seam then up the face past one bolt to the top. Belay with gear, walk off descent- left goes all the way to the wash with a short section of 4th class, right and up goes to the top of the Chuckwalla Couloir and is 3rd class down this.# Location
On the arete in the middle of the wall to the left of Shark Fin.# Protection
One bolt, brass nuts, cams up to 3".
