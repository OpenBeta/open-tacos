# Description
Ascend the first dihedral using the crack on the left face to a ledge halfway up. Mantle onto the ledge and continue in the corner pulling a small roof with many cracks at the top. Belay with gear, walk off descent- left goes all the way to the wash with a short section of 4th class, right and up goes to the top of the Chuckwalla Couloir and is 3rd class down this.# Location
The first dihedral on the left end of Shark Fin Wall closest to the wash.# Protection
Stoppers, cams up to 3".
