# Description
Left of "I Did it My Way and right of Charlotte's Web.  Look for a bolt low protecting a smeary high step and then the next bolt is about 20 feet higher.  Follow the bolts to the chains at half a 70 meter rope.# Location
Keyhole out front on the slabs, south of the main entrance through the fence into the canyon. You can park directly in front of the route.# Protection
