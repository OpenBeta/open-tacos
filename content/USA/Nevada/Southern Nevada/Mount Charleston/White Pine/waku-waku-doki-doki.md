# Description
Climb a couple of jugs up to a big left hand gaston...then crimp, gaston and tryhard your way through a pretty hard boulder problem (V4/5) to a sit down rest. Some 5.11 climbing brings you to the chains. Beware the tree. Strong boulder'ers might find this an easily doable 5.12...route climbers may find it hard for 12b.# Location
