# Description
Start behind a large boulder/cave on the tongue of rock that forms the lowest part of the face. Climb past the scrub oak and a bolt to a horizontal crack, then past three more bolts to the top. Belay on a ledge with two bolts.# Location
Low on the east face of Bone Daddy Dome near the middle.# Protection
Three bolts and a 1-2" cam.
