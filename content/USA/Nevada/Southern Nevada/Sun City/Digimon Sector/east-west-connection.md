# Description
Techy face climbing with a tricky finish.# Location
Next route right of Designer Dog (13a). Starts with a tricky bulge to a slab (stick clip recommended). Trends slightly rightward through a vertical face to a set of anchors below a steep headwall. Stop here (12a) or continue through two more bolts to the second set of chains (13a).# Protection
8 or 9 bolts + chains
