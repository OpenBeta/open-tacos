# Description
The crux of this fantastic climb may be encountered in the initial crimpy boulder problem, but don't forget your endurance for the pumpy, technical seams that lead to the anchor.# Location
Twenty feet right of Slampiece, around a black chert-covered bulge. Locate the crimpy boulder problem connecting two large huecos from bolts 1 to 3.# Protection
8 Bolts to Anchors
