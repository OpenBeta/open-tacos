# Description
Climb pockets, popcorn, and tufas up to the low angle corner. Bust out left and into a technical overhanging dihedral. Awesome movement between the steep overhung wall on the right and the thin wall on the left lead to chains.# Location
Start up the low angle corner clipping two shared fixed draws. Follow the bolt line left into the dihedral.# Protection
Bolts.
