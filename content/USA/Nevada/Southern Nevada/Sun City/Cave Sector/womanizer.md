# Description
Intricate and powerful climbing through orange streaked stone. The rock is bullet and the climbing is engaging the entire way. Climbs through undulating scoops and bulges until it merges with Cancer Crack to the left.# Location
On the wall left of the main corner. Look for a bolt line trending leftward to a shared anchor with Cancer Crack.# Protection
Bolts.
