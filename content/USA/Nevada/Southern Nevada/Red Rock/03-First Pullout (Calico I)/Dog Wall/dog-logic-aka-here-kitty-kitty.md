# Description
Climbs a vertical to gently overhanging wall on good edges to anchors at the lip of the wall. This was the second bolted line on the wall; it was orinally a toprope problem but soon after bolted.Listed in current guidebooks as Here Kitty, Kitty and rated 5.11c.# Location
Located in the center of the wall, this is the third bolted line from the left.# Protection
4 bolts, anchors (3/8" & 1/2" SS)
