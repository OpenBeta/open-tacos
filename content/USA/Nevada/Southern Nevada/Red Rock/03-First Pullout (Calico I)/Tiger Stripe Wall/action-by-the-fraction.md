# Description
Start to the left of the scrub oak in the gully.  Boulder up to the bolt, then traverse right to the seam which is followed to the anchor.# Location
This is the second route from the left.# Protection
Pro to 2 Camalot.  Emphasis on small stuff.  one pro bolt, bolted anchor.
