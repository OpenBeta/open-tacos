# Description
Easy approach, barely used climbing area.# Location
This route top ropes the front face of the pillar to the right of Viagra Corner.# Protection
No bolts. Top rope class 3 scramble to anchor. Two hard to reach bolts just below the top of the pillar are used for anchor.
