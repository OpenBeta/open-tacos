# Description
This route is located on the south facing wall in the slot to the left of the main crag. The rightmost line; reach across to two huecos and climb over a little roof up the varnish slab to two juggy flakes. Use these to pull the overhang and arrive at the anchors.# Location
# Protection
4 bolts to anchors.
