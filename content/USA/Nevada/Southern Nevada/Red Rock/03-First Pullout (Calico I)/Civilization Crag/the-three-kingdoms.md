# Description
Climbs to the highest point on the southwest face via some slab, some near vertical climbing on large varnish plates and jugs, with a large break about halfway up. Climbs like a gym climb.# Location
Starts about 10 feet left of an obvious crack and flake system; the middle bolted line on the southwest face of the formation.# Protection
Eight bolts to chains.
