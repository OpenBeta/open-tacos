# Description
Tricky slab start or traverse in from the left on the ledge on top of the slab.# Location
Right most bolted climbing line. Shares the anchor with Tea Cups and Tilt-A-Whirl.# Protection
3 bolts to shared hook anchor. Drop your rope into the hooks and top rope/lower directly.
