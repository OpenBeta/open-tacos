# Description
Top rope climbing line on the right side of the wall. Use slab to start or traverse in from the left on the ledge above the slab.# Location
Right side of the wall, 8th climbing line from the left and directly under the shared anchor. Shares with Tea Cups and Roller Coaster.# Protection
Top rope
