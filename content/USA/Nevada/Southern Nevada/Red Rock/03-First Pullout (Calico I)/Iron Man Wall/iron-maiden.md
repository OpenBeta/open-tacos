# Description
Climbs the obvious line on the right side of the main wall. Third class to a balancy step over to the first bolt leads to a small bulge. A couple more balancy moves pull up into the beginning of hueco juggy goodness. Climb the huecos to the short, steep face with sharp, positive edges; committing moves all the way to the chains.# Location
