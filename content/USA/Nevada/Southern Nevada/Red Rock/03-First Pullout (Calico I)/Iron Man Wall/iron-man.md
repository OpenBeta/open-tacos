# Description
Climbs the primary flake and crack system on the main wall. Start near the right facing corner on the left side of the wall, angling up and right under a small roof. Clip a single bolt, pull the roof, and continue up an right until you reach a small crack. Drop in a cam, pull up and over this second little roof, and angle back left to finish on easier terrain. A bit sandy in places.# Location
# Protection
Single Rack to 2.5". One bolt. No anchors. Walk off.
