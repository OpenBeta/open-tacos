# Description
Face climb on a small dislocated boulder. Nice technical moves on crimps with good feet. A bit too short.# Location
The route is 30 feet left of Dolphin Safe.# Protection
You can reach the two bolt anchor by scrambling up the buttress from the left. The anchor is visible from the ground.
