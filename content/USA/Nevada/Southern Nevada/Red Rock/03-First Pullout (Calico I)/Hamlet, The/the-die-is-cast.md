# Description
This route starts under a small roof on the upper Hamlet. It is the pull of this roof , the routes crux , that garners the majority of the 5.9+ grade (estimated a 5.9+/10a move by the FA), the climbing that follows is solid 5.7 on terrain similar to its neighbors to the right. A longer draw is recommended for the first bolt.# Location
The fist bolt is alone under the small roof directly to the left of Frailty thy name is Sandstone on the upper part of The Hamelet# Protection
5 bolts to 2 bolt anchor with chains.
