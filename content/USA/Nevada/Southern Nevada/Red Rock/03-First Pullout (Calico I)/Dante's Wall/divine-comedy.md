# Description
One of the best sport climbs of its grade in Red Rock. Climb the slightly slabby first half on wonderfully solid varnish crimps to a nice rest, then climb the dead vertical second half around a discontinuous crack to the anchors.# Location
# Protection
Seven bolts to chains.
