# Description
This route is located at the extreme right (north) end of the wall. Follow a large curving flake past a bolt, over a bulge - crux -, past three more bolts to bolted anchors. This route shares anchors with Mission Accomplished.# Location
As with all routes on this wall, you can get to the anchors by simply walking up the left (south) end of the formation. The base of the climb starts on top of the ledge at the  far right (north) end of the wall.# Protection
Four bolts to bolted anchors.
