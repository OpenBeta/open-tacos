# Description
This route follows scattered and somewhat discontinuous crack systems over a featured face. The gear placements are not necessarily beginner friendly and can be somewhat tricky.# Location
Just to the right of Dinosaur Omelet is a heavily featured face with scatted cracks and fissures the general left side of this face below the routes finishing anchor is the line.# Protection
Standard rack , 2 bolt anchor at top.
