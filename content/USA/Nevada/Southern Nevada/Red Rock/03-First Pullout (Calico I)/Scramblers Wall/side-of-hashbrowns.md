# Description
This route runs though a swath or cracks towards a two bolt anchor. While the climbing is mellow the gear placements can be tricky and not overly beginner friendly.# Location
Just to the left of Scrambled Salami is a face heavily featured with many meandering crack systems the route runs up the general right side of this face toward a two bolt anchor.# Protection
Standard rack , Two bolt anchor at top.
