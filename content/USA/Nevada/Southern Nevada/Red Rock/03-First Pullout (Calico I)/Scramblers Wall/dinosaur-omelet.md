# Description
The crux of this route is above the chimney where it gets flat.# Location
The route is about 50 feet to the left of Scrambled Salami and it starts with a chimney that is about 1 foot wide. There is a walk-off that leads down to a trail that takes you back to the base climb.# Protection
The size of the protection is .75 to 5 inches. At the top there are bolted anchors.
