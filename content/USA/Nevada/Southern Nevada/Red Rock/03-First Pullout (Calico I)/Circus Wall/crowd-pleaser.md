# Description
Mixed, 3 bolts.  Begin just right of the base of the large arch.  Climb a left leaning crack to a large edge and a bolt.  Take the crack as it angles up passing two more bolts.  Continue finishing just below a large boulder.All info from Red Rock Canyon Climbing Guide Roxanna Brock.# Location
10th route from the left.# Protection
3 bolts.
