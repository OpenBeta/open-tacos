# Description
Climb easily up to the right edge of the roof.  Swing steeply out right on good holds, then make bouldery moves up the wall just right of a bulging seam (3 bolts) until the angle eases and easier climbing leads to an anchor. (from Handren guide)# Location
Immediately right of Main Attraction, on the right edge of the roof# Protection
