# Description
Mixed, 2 bolts.  Climb the face 5 feet right of the previous route, to the left edge of the highest point of the arch feature.  Climb over the roof using 2 bolts and finish of the face above.  This route was the first with bolts placed with a hammer drill in Red Rock Canyon.All info from Red Rock Canyon Climbing Guide Roxanna Brock.# Location
7th route from the left.# Protection
2 bolts.  Gear anchor
