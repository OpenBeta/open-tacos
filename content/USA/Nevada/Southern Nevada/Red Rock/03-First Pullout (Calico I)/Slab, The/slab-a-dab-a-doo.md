# Description
Climbs up through rock quality which provides varying amounts of confidence from hold to hold for the first 2-3 bolts before easing into standard slab fare.# Location
This route is the 7th route from the left. It starts about 12ft to the right ofSlabulousand about 15ft left ofSlab One. It goes up to the first set of chains to the left of the only set of chains on the upper portion of the wall, the chains forSlab One.# Protection
Five bolts lead to a two chain anchor with quick links.
