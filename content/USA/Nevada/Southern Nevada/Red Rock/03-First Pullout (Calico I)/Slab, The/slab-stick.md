# Description
Start about 10ft right ofA Little  Slab'll Do Ya. The crux is on the lower portion and like most of the other routes gets easier from there on.# Location
This is the 4th route from the right and is under the 3rd set of chains to the right of the only set of chains on the upper portion of the wall. It is about 10ft to the right ofA Little  Slab'll Do Yaand 8ft left ofSlab Happyand it shares the anchor with those two routes.# Protection
Five bolts lead to a two chain anchor with quick links.
