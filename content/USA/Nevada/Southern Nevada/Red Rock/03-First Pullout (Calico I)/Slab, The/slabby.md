# Description
"Slabby" is the 4th route from the left, just to the right of Slabracadabra and starts in just about the same place only goes up and to the  right.  Seven bolts lead up to the right 50ft to the two chain anchor that is shared with the next climb to right "Chuckwalla Slab"# Location
The anchor for this route is difficult to see from the ground but is located about midway between the first two chain anchors and the next visible chain anchor to the right.# Protection
Seven bolts lead up to a two chain anchor with quick links.
