# Description
"Chuckwalla Slab" is the 5th route from the left and starts about 12 or 14 feet to the right ofSlabby.  Seven bolts lead up and left 50ft to the two chain anchor that is shared with the previous route,Slabby.# Location
The anchor for this route is difficult to see from the bottom but is located about midway between the first two chain anchors and the third chain anchors that can be seen easily from the ground.# Protection
Seven bolts lead up to a two chain anchor with quick links.
