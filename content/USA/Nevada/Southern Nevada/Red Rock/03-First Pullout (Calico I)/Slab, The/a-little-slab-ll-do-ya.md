# Description
Start at about the same point as the previous route (To Slab or Not). Start up the slab then proceed past the overlap and off to the right to the anchor.# Location
This is the 5th route from the right and is under the 3rd set of chains to the right of the only set of chains on the upper portion of the wall. It starts about 4ft right ofTo Slab or Notand 10ft left ofSlab Stick. Shares the anchor with the next two routes (Slab StickandSlab Happy).# Protection
Five bolts lead to a two chain anchor with quick links.
