# Description
Climb patina face for three bolts leading to a right-trending runout arete scramble for two more bolts.# Location
On the small buttress separating American Ninja from Out of Control, on the East (Out of Control) side face.# Protection
Six bolts. This might supplement with gear, but the climbing is so easy, even the most timid gym leader will find it ok to just run out.Finishes on a two-bolt anchor, or optionally do two more moves and rap from the American Ninja anchors.
