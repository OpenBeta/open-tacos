# Description
low angle climbing on featured, black flakes. good fun; lots of sun.# Location
the center of the black face, climber's right on the approach gulley to Shadow Play and Promote the Segregation; start about 100 feet up the gulley, before it widens, where a horizontal crack/weakness intersects the gulley. go up, roughly following a crack/weakness to the right end of the belay ledge. walk off climber's left and back down the gulley to the base.# Protection
standard rack; the anchor takes #3 and #4 camalots
