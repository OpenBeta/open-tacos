# Description
a respectable wide crack; don't let the 15 feet of sandy start deter you. 200 feet of camelot 4, 5, 6, & 7 climbing in good rock. (I know, no #7 camalot; but if there were, you'd use it...)# Location
in the alcove across the canyon from Stick Right & Left, the wide, parallel sided crack right of the trees on the face# Protection
wide rack; a couple of camalot 2s & 3s & some smaller will supplement the big stuff; rappel anchor up & left of topout. possible intermediate anchor at middle tree.
