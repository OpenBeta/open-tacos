# Description
Up low-angle slabs to the black, jumbled system of corners; up these to a short OW section; surmount a chockstone and hike back 40 feet to belay at the base of a wide crack system that goes up and then arches right. Up the crack and out right to the big ledge. Rappel from bushes or 3rd/4th class left to hiking.# Location
the jumbled black face left of The Stick/Slip Phenomenon# Protection
normal rack + a #5 camalot
