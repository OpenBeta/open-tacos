# Description
Climb the obvious thin crack. Being a good face climber is a good idea for this one. The runout section is at the bottom and comes in around 5.10-.# Location
To the right of Bush pilots and the left of varnishing point. Fungus folks climbs the very obvious seam up the perfect varnished face. There is more gear then it appears from the ground.# Protection
Small nuts and cams. Shares an anchor with bush pilots.
