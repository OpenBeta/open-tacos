good route with five bolts and gear in between.Stemming and technical balancey climbing.# Location
left of the brass wall.  Locate a bolt in a low, broken band of rock below a shallow dihedral.# Protection
5 bolts with gear in between some good some marginal.  there is just enough gear to make the route safe.  Not for the beginner 5.11 leader.
