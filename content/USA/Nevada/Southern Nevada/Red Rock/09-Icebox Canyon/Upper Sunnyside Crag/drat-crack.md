# Description
Continue past Sunnyside Crag, enter the drainage of Ice Boxhead up the canyon - look on the right side for two beautiful black cracks about 50 ft above the creek bed. Crack on the right is Drat Crack.  5.8 climbing leads to a natural anchor - slings through the rock.# Location
# Protection
Standard rack up to #4.Two ropes to rap.
