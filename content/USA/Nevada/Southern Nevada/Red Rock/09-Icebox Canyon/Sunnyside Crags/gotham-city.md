# Description
Start at a steep, pocketed face just behind a tree. The climb is steep and follow discontinuous cracks up the face. Once the angle kicks back, head up and left to the fixed anchors above Spring Break.# Location
Begin 5 feet left of Mister Masters and 5 feet right of Spring Break, just behind a tree.# Protection
Small gear up to a #2 Friend.
