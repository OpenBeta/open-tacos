# Description
The crux is mid way a few moves above ledge in right slanting crack. Great exposure on steep varnished wall. Once at the top you can continue up and left to gain the anchors for Mercedes and top rope it. (if your so inclined)# Location
Rappel with 60 meter rope.# Protection
Standard gear to 3.5 camolot
