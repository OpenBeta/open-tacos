# Description
Just above the Necromancer crag is an obvious orange wall with a striking corner on the right hand side- This is the route. A dirty pitch up the  brushy gully leads to the base of the main crack, then a long pitch leads to a ledge far up the corner.# Location
Just above Necromancer.# Protection
Good selection to 5", heavy in the thin hands to fists.
