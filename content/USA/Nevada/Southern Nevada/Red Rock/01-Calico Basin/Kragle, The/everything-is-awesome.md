# Description
Climb the steep overhanging crack for 10ft until it pulls over a bulge and the angle eases off. Continue up the crack line through some loose rock, and finish at the anchors up and right of the crack line.# Location
Crack 10ft right of the box chimney that is Double Decker Couch. 50ft to the right of the prominent, left-facing corner landmarking the crag.# Protection
Single rack with #4 to really protect the start. Ring anchors up top.
