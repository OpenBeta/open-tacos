# Description
Climb up the center of the steep face through the body-sized patch of white rock next to the left-facing flake. There are two distinct cruxes: a mantle over a bulge down low and a big reach on crimps above the flake. Top out to the anchors.# Location
Second route to the right of the big, left-facing corner on the steep face. Start on the ground next to the ledges that go up into the corner. See topo photo.# Protection
9 bolts to ring anchors
