# Description
Follow the bolts up the arete that lies on the right of Bottoms Up.Mostly mellow (5.9/5.10-?) climbing with a 5.11- crux in the middle where the holds disappear. There's a bolt where you want it.# Location
Rightmost bolted line on Gnat Man Crag -- the first climb you'll see if you follow the directions for the Gnat Man Crag approach on Mountain Project. Between the left-facing Bottoms Up corner and the obvious gully.# Protection
Bolts (6? I might be misremembering) to bolted anchors
