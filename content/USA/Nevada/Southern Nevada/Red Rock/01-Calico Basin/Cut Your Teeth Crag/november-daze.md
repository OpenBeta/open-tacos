# Description
Mark put this line up on lead.  It is fun face climbing with numerous holds and great views.# Location
This line is about 50 feet from the west edge of Cut Your Teeth Crag.  Mike McGlynn and Todd Lane put up a new line about 8 feet right of this route.  It is called September Knights.# Protection
9 bolts to chains.
