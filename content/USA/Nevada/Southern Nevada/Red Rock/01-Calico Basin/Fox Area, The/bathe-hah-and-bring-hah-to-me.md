# Description
Climb a varnished corner and dirty slab to beneath the large roof. Jam and undercling rightward out the roof and up to the top. Many flaky face holds make this route much easier than it appears from the ground.The top anchor takes a variety of cams.# Location
This climbs the huge left-facing corner on the right side of the crag, about 20 feet right of Cry With the Saints, and 120 feet right of The Fox.# Protection
1ea. #1 TCU - #4 Camalot2ea. #.5 - #2 Camalot
