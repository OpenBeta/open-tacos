# Description
Overhanging face which climbs out a series of roofs before the crux and up the head wall. The climbing is not straight forward as you use slopers, pockets, edges, and heel hooks.# Location
The route is the first route left of the main chimney (5.8) as you are looking directly at the wall.  Start under the series of roofs and lower off of the anchors at the top.# Protection
The route is well protected clipping six bolt en route to the fixed anchors.
