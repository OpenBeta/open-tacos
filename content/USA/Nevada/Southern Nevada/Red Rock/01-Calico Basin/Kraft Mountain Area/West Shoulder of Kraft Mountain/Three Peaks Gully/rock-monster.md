# Description
This route tackles the thin roof that leads up to good jams and to the wide crack.  Fun to the top.# Location
This route is located on the east side of South Peak (the first of the three little peaks, it will be on your left side as you face uphill.)It is a prominent crack in brown stone that widens and leans to the right.# Protection
Trad gear. Walk off to east.
