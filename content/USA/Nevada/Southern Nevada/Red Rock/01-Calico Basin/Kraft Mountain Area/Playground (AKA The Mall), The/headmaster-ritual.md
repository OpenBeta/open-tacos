# Description
Fairly sustained and pumpy crimping on the varnished vertical face.This route has the appearance of being a complete pile, but it climbs rather well. This takes the rightmost bolted line on the right face of the upper tear of the Mall.# Location
Straight up the middle of the large boulder sitting at the top of the hillside.# Protection
4 bolts
