# Description
Climb past first bolt to low-angle face with cracks.Route may be supplemented with gear at this point or run it out to next bolt. Wall steepens at this point, up and a move to the left(crux area) and then on up to anchors.# Location
This the second bolted route down gully from the top. On obvious cream colored steep face.# Protection
Bolts and anchors.Small cams,nuts optional.
