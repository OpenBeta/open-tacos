# Description
Overhanging crux start pulls up to a crumbly ledge, then traverse 4 feet right and climb straight up the face (5.9) staying a few feet to the left of the crack.  Avoid the lip of the crack on the right.# Location
Starts 9 feet left of "From Soup to Nuts" and 8 feet right of "Singing Love Pen".# Protection
Shares trad anchor with "From Soup to Nuts".  Walk to the top and set up a trad anchor for TR, or climb "From Soup to Nuts" 5.7 and use that anchor.
