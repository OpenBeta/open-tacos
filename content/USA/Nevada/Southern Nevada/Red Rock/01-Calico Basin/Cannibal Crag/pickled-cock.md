# Description
A very fun route that includes some footwork and fun moves on slightly overhanging rock.# Location
Most people climb up and right to the first bolt from the same start asSave the Heart to Eat Later, which begins on the raised ledge (or just right of it). Continue traversing up and right to the third bolt, where you can either make a short leftward zig-zag or continue straight up toward the forth bolt. Continue up to the chains.# Protection
4 bolts to the Chain anchors.
