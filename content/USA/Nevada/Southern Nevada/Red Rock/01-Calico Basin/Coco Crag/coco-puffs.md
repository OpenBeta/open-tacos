# Description
Right of Stupid Cat and left of Fontare De Roja.Traverse left from the boulder on good pockets with smeary feet, then climb the crack to the shared alcove, and finally up the face to an anchor (2 bolts).# Location
# Protection
Single rack to BD #1, several slings, 2 quickdraws. A 60m rope will reach for the rappel.
