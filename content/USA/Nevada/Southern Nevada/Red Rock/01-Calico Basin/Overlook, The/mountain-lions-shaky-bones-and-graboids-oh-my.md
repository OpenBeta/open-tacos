# Description
Overcome the challenging start and enjoy easier climbing for the remainder of the route. You can expect a consistent 10-12' between bolts.# Location
The start is about 10' to the right of a slender slot (separating the two walls) and the main wall's (right angling) left arete/rib.# Protection
8 bolts w/ a bolted, chain anchor. 60 meter rope at a minimum, knot the end.
