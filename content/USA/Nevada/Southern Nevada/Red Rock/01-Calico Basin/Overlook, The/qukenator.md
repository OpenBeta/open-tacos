# Description
Overcome the challenging start and enjoy easier climbing for the remainder of the route. You can expect a consistent 10-12' between bolts.Sounds like; Q-kenator.# Location
Start about 10' right of Mountain Lions, 10' left of the Behind The Line crack and just left of a small scrub oak bush.# Protection
9 bolts w/ a bolted, chain anchor. 60 meter rope at a minimum, knot the end.
