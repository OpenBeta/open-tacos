# Description
Start on a sloping ledge, working up the corner until it begins to get steeper.  Look for feet and begin lay backing or jaming to round over the overhang. From here follow a horizontal hand crack right and finish up on Brother Bill's Great Thrill.Continuing after the slung block anchor eventually takes you to the upper pitches of One-Arm Bandit.# Location
The right facing corner on a wall of varnished stone to the left of Olive Oil.# Protection
Single rack to 2", emphasis .3 -.5,  optional 5"
