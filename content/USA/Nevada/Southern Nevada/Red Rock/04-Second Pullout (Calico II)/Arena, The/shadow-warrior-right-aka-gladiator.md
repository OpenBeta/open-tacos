# Description
Climb large hueco jugs to a shelf and traverse left to the edge of the shelf. Move up and left through some crimps, including one glued to the wall, to join Shadow Warrior post-crux at its 4th bolt. Finish up Shadow Warrior with a mix of crimps and jugs.Stick clip or skip the first bolt. You can't reach it from the holds.# Location
# Protection
Bolts to a lower-off anchor.
