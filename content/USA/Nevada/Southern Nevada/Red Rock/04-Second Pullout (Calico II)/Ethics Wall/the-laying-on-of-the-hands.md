# Description
The climbing gets more difficult the higher you go.# Location
Begin behind a large cube of rock, about 15' right of Rafter Man.# Protection
8 bolts: There are two bolts protecting the smooth face, followed by 3 more bolts leading up the honeycombed rock to the base of the red band, with 3 more bolts heading up and left on the overhanging rock.
