# Description
Take the lower deck and wander behind a scrub oak bush.  The start is at a deep hueco.  The route climbs steep varnished rock using scoops & thin edges.# Location
4th route from the right side of the wall.  Behind a scrub oak# Protection
8 bolts to chain anchors
