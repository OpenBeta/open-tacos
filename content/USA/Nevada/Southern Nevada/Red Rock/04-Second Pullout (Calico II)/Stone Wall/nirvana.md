# Description
The start of this route is technical and uses a left facing flake which soon brings you to a right facing flake.  Many edges and seems with positive yet steep holds will bring you to the bolted summit.# Location
Middle of the wall - 4th route from the left.# Protection
7 bolts to shut bolted anchor
