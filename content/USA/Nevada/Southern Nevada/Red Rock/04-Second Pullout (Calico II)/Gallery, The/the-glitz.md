# Description
This beautiful line traverses the overhang at the far end of the Gallery, a few climbs to the right of The Gift. Hand over hand movement along a fantastic rail leads to a dynamic move followed shortly thereafter by a ginormous hueco rest. Continue up to solid anchors on the same face# Location
