# Description
This route starts out climbing a right leaning crack  (crux) and continues through some large huecos and ledges.  In the Handren guidebook, this route is referred to as "Need to Rest," but Mike's original name was Idiot Parade.# Location
This is the last route on the right side of the lower corridor, if you enter from the bottom. It is located just before the dividing boulders.# Protection
5 bolts up to new chains.
