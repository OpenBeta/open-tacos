# Description
A spectacular line that lies about 15' left of Marshall Amp- a powerful start leads to fun and varied climbing above. Stick clipping the first bolt may be a good idea if this is at your limit!# Location
Just left of Marshall Amp- the obvious horn at head height is the starting hold.# Protection
9 bolts/anchors
