# Description
Pleasant jamming up the obvious hand crack to a ledge.  Move left on a seam from the ledge and follow easy face moves to the top.# Location
On the north-facing section of the Meister's Edge area, 75 feet right of the prominent, right-leaning crack of Johnny Rotten (5.11a).# Protection
Standard Rack to 4".  Finger sized crack takes nuts/cams for top anchor.  Walk off by scrambling all the way west.
