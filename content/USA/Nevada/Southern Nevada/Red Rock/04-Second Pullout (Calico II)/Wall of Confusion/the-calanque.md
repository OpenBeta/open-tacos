# Description
This route begins opposite Makulu and Super Guide up in the alcove at the right end of the Wall of Confusion. The first half of the route climbs interesting features up a vertical face to a break beneath a steeper headwall. From this break, follow the left line of bolts to a bolted anchor.This route has an alarming number of portable holds but it will likely improve with more traffic.# Location
# Protection
10 or so bolts.
