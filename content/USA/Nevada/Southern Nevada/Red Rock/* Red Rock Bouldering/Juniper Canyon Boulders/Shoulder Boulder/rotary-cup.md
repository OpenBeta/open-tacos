# Description
Begin on a great rail in the middle of the face, right at eye level. Move up on good crimps to a right hand gaston, and lunge for the glory jug at the lip. Top out up and to the left.# Location
The climb ascends the slight overhang on the left side of the southern face.# Protection
Pad or two
