Fun, easy route requiring you to squirm through a hole at the top of the tunnel.# Location
Stand start by the bat-hang of Darwin Award in the center of the tunnel created by the flat boulder leaning on Monkey Bar Boulder.  Climb up and wriggle through the hole!  Weight dependent. You can also start using the same start as Darwin Award# Protection
