# Description
This route is located on the back side of the Poker Chip (Beth Wald boulder in the Southern Nevada Bouldering) guidebook.  Stand start on slabby face to small crimpy holds and make cool moves to progressively better holds# Location
Back side of the Poker Chip Boulder, to the right of the V5 face and to the left of the very low angle slab# Protection
