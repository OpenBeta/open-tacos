# Description
Sit start with two crimps and options for feet, move up and right on crimps into the seam/crack on the right side of the boulder for the topout.# Location
On the right side of the uphill side of the Plumber's Crack boulder, left of the downclimb. Sit start down and ~4 feet left of the vertical crack/seam in the boulder.# Protection
Pads, spotter.
