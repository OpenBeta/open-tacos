# Description
Start as per The Wave and traverse the rail left. At the transition off the rail, stay low and move left only using the holds underneath the lip of the roof. Manage your way over to a good left slot and top out over the left side of the bulge.# Location
Same start as The Wave on the large rail.# Protection
Pads.
