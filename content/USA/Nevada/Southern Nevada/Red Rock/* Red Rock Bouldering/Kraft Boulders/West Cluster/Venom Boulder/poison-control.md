# Description
Arete to the right of Spittin' Venom. Start on a nice crimp that has a mono pocket along it and the lowest mono-pocket on the right-hand side. Pull yourself off the ground, crank into as many of the crimps possible until you hit the arete and top out.Guide book rates it @ around V9 so starting there.# Location
# Protection
A pad or two and a spotter.
