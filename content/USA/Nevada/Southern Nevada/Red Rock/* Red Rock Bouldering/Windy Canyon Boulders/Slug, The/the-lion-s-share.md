# Description
A beautiful climb with great holds and the main attraction of the Slug boulder. Stand start with a small, positive right underling and left hand on poor slimper of the rail above. Work up and right to a difficult deadpoint before turning the corner and taking the slab up and left.# Location
Obvious brown varnished swath of rock on the north face of the Slug boulder# Protection
