# Description
Great movement on good rock. Begin with your left hand on a flat crimp side pull and your right hand on the arete. Make your way up the face, staying to the left of the arete, until you reach the bucket jug at the lip.# Location
Begin on the left side of the prominent overhanging arete.# Protection
2 pads minimum, 4 pads is optimal
