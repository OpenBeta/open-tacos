# Description
Sit start in the middle of the bow (front) of the hull using an obvious flat shelf. Make a big move to a decent right hand followed by a series of moves up the middle of the bow to a shelf you can stand up on. Finish the climb by mantling up the flat face to the crow’s nest.# Location
Dead center of the bow (front) of the hull. To the left of Finding Nemo, right of Nautilus.# Protection
1-2 pads
