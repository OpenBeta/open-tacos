# Description
Pork Chops Boulder:  Large prominent bouldersstart on elevated platform.  Sit start on lower Right rail.  Climb the rail to reach jugs located in the middle of the boulder.  Heel hooks are great fun.# Location
From Blood trails, look up the canyon (northwest) to find a prominent boulder with flat base and another large vertical "flat iron" boulder resting against it. Behold, the Sundial Boulder!# Protection
Exposure due to elevated platform. good spotter will ensure good times!
