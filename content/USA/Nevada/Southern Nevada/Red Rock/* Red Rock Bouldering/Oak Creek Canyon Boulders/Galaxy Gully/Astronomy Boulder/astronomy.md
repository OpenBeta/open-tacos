# Description
Sit or crouch start with a good jug on the right side of the arete. Work around left to establish on the face, and continue with better holds to the lip. Exit slightly left of the lip.# Location
A very short walk uphill from the Lunar boulder.# Protection
2 or more pads and a spotter for the top-out.
