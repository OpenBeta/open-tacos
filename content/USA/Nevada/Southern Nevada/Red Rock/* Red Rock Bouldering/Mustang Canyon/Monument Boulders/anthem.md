# Description
A picturesque square-cut overhanging aréte lies in the heart of the main cluster of the Monument Boulders.# Location
Large boulder right beside Nana Nana. Sit-start low on underclings then power and finesse your way along the overhanging aréte and pull over the lip at the end to top out.# Protection
3+ pads
