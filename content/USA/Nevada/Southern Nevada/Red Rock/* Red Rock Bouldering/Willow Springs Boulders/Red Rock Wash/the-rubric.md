# Description
An intimidating highball. Climb up under the right edge of the roof skirting around it and follow the seam to the top. Better be able to onsight v3/4 if you give this one a shot.# Location
The Rubric is on a large boulder with a huge roof a few hundred yards past Mothers Crag into the Red Rock Wash. About one hundred yards before (north of) the "Ride The White Horse" boulder, on the west side of the wash# Protection
Dont fall!
