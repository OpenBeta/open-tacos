# Description
Standing, split start with high left hand and low right. Use small crack to work towards arete then reach high jug across the gap with your right hand for the crux. Move left hand up then use the crack to top out.# Location
Start on far left of boulder (south side). Moves towards crack/gap in center and then up.# Protection
Crash pad(s)
