# Description
Sit start on left hand undercling and a right hand sidepull, climb through compression, make a big move to the nose pinch/meatwrap, throw up a high heel and make a big move to the lip.# Location
Hike on the main trail for about 20 minutes then when you reach a fork, stay left down into the wash and the boulder will be right on the wash.# Protection
3-4 pads
