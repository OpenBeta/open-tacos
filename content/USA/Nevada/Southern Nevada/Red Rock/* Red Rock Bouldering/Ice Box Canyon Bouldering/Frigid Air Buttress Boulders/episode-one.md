# Description
Start with your left hand on the incut crimp that your right hand would be on if you were about to send The Force is with You, and a right hand on a finger jug. Work your way up to the flake with great holds once this is gained. The top-out is simple with good holds all day.# Location
The right route on the Tri-force boulder. A boulder previously described in The Force is with You.# Protection
Simple v1 climbing skils or maybe a pad if you want mental pro.
