# Description
A quality alternative to the first pitch of Ixtlan. Start 20 feet right of Ixtlan, following 6 bolts through a tricky/insecure left-facing corner, two small roofs, and a thin final section up then traversing left to the Ixtlan pitch 1 anchor.# Location
Other side of the bush.# Protection
Bolts, optional smallish placement before the first bolt.
