# Description
Follows the seam atop the boulder just left of Go Ahead and Jump to its apex, then juggy varnish to the rap tree. Gear is spotty but the rock is good and the climbing is fun.# Location
Starts atop the boulder just left of GA+J, follows it rightwards to hueco-y finish.# Protection
