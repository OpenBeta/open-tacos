# Description
Climb the crack up to a large ledge.  Building an anchor is tough, but you can get waist-deep in a slot between the wall the crack is in and the main wall.  There is a small tree that can be slung.Traverse left from the top of the climb to a large chimney with a rap tree.# Location
This route follows the first crack to the left of Kemosabe and is just to the left of Dense Dunce and just to the right of Go Ahead and Jump.# Protection
medium gear for the climb, some ingenuity for the anchor
