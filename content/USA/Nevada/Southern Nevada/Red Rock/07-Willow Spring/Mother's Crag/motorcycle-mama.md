# Description
Motorcycle Mama is the giant left-facing corner on the left side of the crag. 90'. beautiful, unlikely left facing corner. follow the corner using jams, liebacks, and face climbing. very imaginative despite the grade.  5 stars!Joanne O'Brien is my mom and she drives a Harley Davidson, happy mother's day mom!# Location
Obvious corner.  Utilize the rappel station from the top of the climb.  single 60 meter rope rappel.# Protection
single rack to 3''. optional 5. include smallest c3 or rp's in your rack.
