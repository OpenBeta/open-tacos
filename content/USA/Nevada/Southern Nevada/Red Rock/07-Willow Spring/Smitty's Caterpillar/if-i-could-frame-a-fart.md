# Description
Climb a black slab at a right facing v-slot (not the bushy crack to the right). At it's top, a steep layback will lead to jugs and a flat podium. Angle up and right across the white face, aiming for a vertical crack running through the varnished caterpillar. To the top.# Location
The route is closer to the left hand side of the formation's east face. Start right of a big pillar and at the intersection of it and the main wall. Walk off to the south.# Protection
Standard single rack. Runners. Build an anchor on top.
