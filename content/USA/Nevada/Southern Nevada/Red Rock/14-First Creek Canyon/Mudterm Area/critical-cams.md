# Description
Just to the right of Mudterm, there is a square-cut corner.  The crack gradually squeezes down from chimney to fist.  This is the route.  To descend, scramble to the right (west).# Location
# Protection
A big cam or two (e.g. number 4.5 and 5 Camalots) is critical.
