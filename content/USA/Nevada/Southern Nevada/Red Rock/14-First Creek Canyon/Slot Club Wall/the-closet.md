# Description
The Closet is a long and sustained squeeze chimney with a few faces holds intermixed to help you out.  The rock is featured on the outside but is often fragile. The rock on the inside of the crack is mostly solid.Stop at an intermediate bolted anchor at about a 100' or continue squeezing upwards for another 80' and top out to walk off to the West.  Choose yer own adventure...# Location
The current left-most route at the Slot Club Wall# Protection
#6, multiple Big Bros or equivalent
