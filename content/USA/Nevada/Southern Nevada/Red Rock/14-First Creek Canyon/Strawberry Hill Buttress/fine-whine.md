# Description
Climb the obvious corner and crack system above, eventually reaching the large ledge midway up the western wall.  Continue across the ledge and up a crack to a belay position by a short chimney.  Climb up the crack and traverse left at a large prominent varnish patch, then straight up to the summit.Walk west to the rappel tree.# Location
Scramble up the approach slabs to the brushy ledge at the base of the western wall.  The route starts in the corner system on the left.# Protection
We had a big cam (#6 Friend) and used it; two ropes for rappel
