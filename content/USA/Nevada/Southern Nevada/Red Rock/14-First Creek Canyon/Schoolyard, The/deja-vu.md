# Description
Fifty yards to the right (west) of Coffin Corner is a smaller right-facing corner. A boulder move over the initial bulge takes you to perfect hand-jams in the corner. At the top of the corner, continue up delicate rock to a small ledge and rappel from a slung chockstone.# Location
