# Description
Friendship Route is the rightmost corner on the Alcohol Wall.P1 (110 feet, 5.8) Follow the corner to a two bolt anchor. The wide crack looks low angle, but is actually a little awkward.P2 (40 feet, 5.9) Continue up the corner, stemming on balls, past a bolt. Belay on the massive ledge from a bush.Walk east on the ledge and rappel with two ropes from the two bolt anchor atop Rob Roy.# Location
