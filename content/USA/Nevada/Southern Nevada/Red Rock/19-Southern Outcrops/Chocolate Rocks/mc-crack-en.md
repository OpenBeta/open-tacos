# Description
This route starts on a fun little flake and goes up a corner to a roof and over the top of the feature to a sling anchor.Rappel the route with one rope.# Location
This route is located in the next corner to the left of Peanut Butter and Jam, on the right margin of Chocolate Rocks.# Protection
Standard rack.
