# Description
A nice little route that will get your blood flowing. Slightly tricky to protect and it gets quite steep about half way up. Fun.Rappel from the sling anchor atop Mc-Crack-En with one rope.# Location
Shoot up the crack on the fin between the Zacker Cracker gully on the left and the Mc-Crack-En area.# Protection
Standard rack.
