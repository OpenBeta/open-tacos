# Description
Another steep offering from Joe Herbst. Take the crack up to an off-width.Rappel the face to the right from the two bolt anchor on Shortcake with one rope.# Location
On the left side of the Chocolate Rocks, the next crack to the right of Combination Corner and to the left of the arete.# Protection
Standard rack plus some wider gear up to #5 Camalot.
