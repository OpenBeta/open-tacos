# Description
Avoids lower seam by a scramble up+right, then back left along a ledge. From there straight up crack to sling anchor, then past bush to obvious right-leaning crack in upper varnish. Follow this to a bolted anchor.# Location
Tall, shady, varnished wall around to the right of Who Deany vicinity. Anchor is visible just left of a wide crack just above a horizontal break a little over 100' up.# Protection
Double cams helpful.5-3
