Climb a tight dihedral starts with some good hand jams end with fingers stem your way up.  Build an anchor up top save some mid sized cams.# Location
Right of False Perception Left of Sensible an obvious black corner.  Rappel from a tree to the right of route.# Protection
Standard up to #2 some extra mid size for anchor
