# Description
First Lady of Magic climbs the corner that curves to the left forming the arch 5 feet right of Who Deany. Ascend the corner until you can reach right and pull up, on to the face. Climb up to a bolt and then head right a bit to a small ledge. Fire up the obvious crack to a ledge with a 2 bolt anchor. Rappel with two ropes.A single rope rappel can be done to the anchors on top of Who Deany to the climber's left, followed by another single rope rap to the ground.# Location
# Protection
Gear up to #2 Camalot. Lots of small gear.
