# Description
Alcove in distinct left facing corner.  Crux is getting up through the alcove by trudging, stemming, chimneying until you can emerge to the corner above.  A bit strenuous but great fun.Rappel from slings with single rope.# Location
# Protection
Takes cams to protect the initial awkward moves in the alcove.  There are slings and rap rings to rap the route.
