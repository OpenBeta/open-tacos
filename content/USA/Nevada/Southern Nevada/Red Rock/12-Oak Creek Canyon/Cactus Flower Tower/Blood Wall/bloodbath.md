# Description
This route follows the prominent corner and crack system on the right (western) edge of the Blood Wall.  Four pitches up the crack lead to easier, broken rock near the summit.  The crux is a steep section on the second pitch.# Location
Scramble up the Blood Wall approach until you can move right (west) to the base of the route.# Protection
Std rack, small cams, wires
