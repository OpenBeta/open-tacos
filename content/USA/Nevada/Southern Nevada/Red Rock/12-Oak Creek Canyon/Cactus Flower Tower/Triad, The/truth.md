# Description
Truth starts in the steep hand to fist/wide crack that leads to the entrance of a massive chimney chasm.  Place your lucky piece and begin the long voyage up the chimney. Follow it until it eventually tightens and squeezes you out to easy stemming to the top.  A long pitch.Belay off slung block or tree and descend by rapping Beauty with a 70m.# Location
The huge V cut crack in the center of the "triad" wall.# Protection
one hand sized, a few fist size, big cams, bro(s), or run out
