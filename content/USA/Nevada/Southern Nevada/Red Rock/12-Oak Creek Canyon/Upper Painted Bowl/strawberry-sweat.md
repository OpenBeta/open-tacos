# Description
Climb easy rock up the crest.  Above a small bush, cross left (bolts) to the obvious crack system.  Follow the crack until it bends left, then move right onto the prow and continue to the top.# Location
Scramble up to the Upper Painter Bowl.  Route is on the major buttress on the left.# Protection
