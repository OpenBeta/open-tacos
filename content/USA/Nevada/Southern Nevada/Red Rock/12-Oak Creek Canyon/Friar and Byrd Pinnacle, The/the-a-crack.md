# Description
This route starts in a clean corner crack and continues upwards though varied terrain.  It features good cracks in a variety of sizes, a few edges, and a couple rest stances before encountering a traversing wide crack that is probably the crux. Pull the bulge and continue up the wide crack to the top of the big shared belay ledge.To get down rappel straight down Xyphoid fever with a 60m rope.# Location
Right of Xyphoid Fever scrabble up to a good stance near/under a bush by a corner crack.# Protection
Single rack to 3", one 5"
