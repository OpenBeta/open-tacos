# Description
Beelzebub climbs a steep and clean offwidth that features very few face holds splitting a beautifully blank wall. One of the nicest offwidths in Red Rock.# Location
The striking offwidth splitting the center of the wall, just left of Wise Guys Off Size.# Protection
Triple #6, single #5. (optional additional #6's)
