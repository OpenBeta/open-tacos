# Description
Da Boneyard is the striking and long offwidth that splits a tall varnished tower. Possibly the longest single pitch of its type.  Some flakey rock but quite an adventure still.To descend, do two rappels with a 70m.# Location
Up the hillside from Little Brown, or also about 200 yards downhill from the height of land and center of Rainbow Mountain.# Protection
Single rack to 2.5", double 3"-5", triple 5"-12", the kitchen sink.
