# Description
This pleasant well bolted route on relatively solid rock begins where the walls of the corridor come together in the depths of the corridor.  Stem agains the overhanging wall at your back and work up face holds to reach the first bolt. Continue upward seeking the best combinations of edges to reach the anchor.# Location
From the parking lot at Sandstone Quarry, look for the first corridor right of the Parking Lot wall known as the White Slab.# Protection
8 bolts and 2 for the anchor
