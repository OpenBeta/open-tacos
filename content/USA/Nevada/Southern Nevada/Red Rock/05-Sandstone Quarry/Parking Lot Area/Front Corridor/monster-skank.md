# Description
Perhaps the finest sport climb in Red Rock. Two bolts of 5.9 jugginess lead to the first of many powerful boulder problems. Enjoy the variety (and the mandetory all points off dyno) and grimace through the crimpers on this beautiful classic!# Location
Deepest route on the left side of the corridor, next toSunsplash(5.13b).# Protection
Chain draws to chains.
