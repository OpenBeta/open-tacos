# Description
Crux as you pass a horizontal bulge, bolt protected. Crux is delicate feet and interesting hands. Good traverse and smearing. Protect well past the bulge for your follower.# Location
North (Left) side of Sandy Corridor. Find the long right rising seam on the left wall.  There's a bolt about 10 feet up. traverse the seam using gear to 2".  Take several small pieces.  Belay when the crack runs out or when you find a good belay spot to walk off.  Walk off climber's left.# Protection
Mixed, Pro to 2". Several bolts. Build a belay.
