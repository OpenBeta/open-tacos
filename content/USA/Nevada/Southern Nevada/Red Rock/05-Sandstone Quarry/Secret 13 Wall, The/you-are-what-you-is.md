# Description
25 ft right of Go a Hundred. Start behind little bush on small holds up two bolts then slightly left then straight up to ok rest, then distinct crux. Continue straight up to tricky slab anchor. Best route of its grade at Red Rocks, mega-classic and a must do!!# Location
The rock that says "youarewhatyouis".# Protection
