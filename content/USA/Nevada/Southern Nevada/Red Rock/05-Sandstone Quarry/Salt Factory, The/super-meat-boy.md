# Description
Climb. Use lay backs, pinches, slopers, crimps, an undercling hole. Steep and bouldery. Have fun!# Location
Uphill and to the left of Bandage Girl on the north facing overhang.# Protection
6 quick draws. 4 for the route and 2 for the anchor.
