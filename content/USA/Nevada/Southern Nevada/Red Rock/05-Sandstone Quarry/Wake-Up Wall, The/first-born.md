# Description
Climb to the first bolt and then continue to a small roof and up through the crack system to the anchor.# Location
Start down in the pit to the left of 'Shut Up and Climb.'# Protection
This was put up as a mixed route with three bolts. The guidebook also suggests a selection of large stoppers and a #4 friend.
