# Description
This is the best route on the cliff! A variety of holds and moves will lead you up this route. A bit runout to the first bolt as you climb the slabby bottom. Some people attempt to stick clip the first bolt...# Location
Climb the bolt line to the right of the big hole/hueco on the far left side of the cliff. Start in the hole at the base of the cliff.# Protection
5 bolts to the chains.
