# Description
The second route on this cliff. A bouldery start that shares the first few holds with Presents of Mind, then traverses right into steep climbing on good holds to the anchors.# Location
Just right of Presents of Mind- look for the VERY high first bolt (stick clip mandatory).# Protection
3 bolts, mussy anchor
