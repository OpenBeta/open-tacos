# Description
fun sport climbing in a great setting. traverse right under a big scoop and over the lip to an exposed face.# Location
begin inside of a gully on the right side of the wall. traverse right under a big scoop to the lip and pull over. climb the long vertical slab on good holds to a bolted anchor.# Protection
bolts to a bolted anchor
