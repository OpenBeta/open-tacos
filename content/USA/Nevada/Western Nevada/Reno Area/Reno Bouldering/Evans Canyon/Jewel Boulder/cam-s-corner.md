# Description
SDS.  Left hand on the pinch below the big flake, or if you are tall enough, left hand on the actual flake.  Right hand on the rock area that juts out close to the right arete.  Left and right hands move to the flake and up and away.# Location
Jewel Boulder.  Just inside the right side of the boulder facing the back side/shady side of boulder.# Protection
Crash Pad.
