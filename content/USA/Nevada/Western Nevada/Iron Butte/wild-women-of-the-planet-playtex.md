# Description
A 5.9 face climb protected by four bolts for the first part.  The crux is the first part of the climb.  After the fourth bolt it gets easier.  Carry pro for the last part which is 5.7.  For a more interesting variation, go right after the fourth bolt and climb Grip Master.# Location
Route 1 in the photos. The most obvious landmark is the giant boulder on the slope below.# Protection
Four bolts protect the first part.  Carry pro to two inches for the second.
