# Description
Really short, hard moves to super easy top section.  Crux is from the first to second bolt.# Location
This is on the right side of the butte.  Rolling Crow is on the right, The Wall is on the left.Climb up the obvious roof right of the deck.  Of the two routes going up the roof, this is the one on the left.# Protection
2 bolts plus optional pro to 1.5" for runout 5.6 top section.
