# Description
Jugs to a shallow ledge. A slab move leads to a thick, prominent dyke.  The crux is on the dyke.  After several bolts, traverse left to chain anchors.# Location
Right side of the base of the Shark's Tooth.# Protection
Bolts. Shares chain anchors with Tooth Decay.
