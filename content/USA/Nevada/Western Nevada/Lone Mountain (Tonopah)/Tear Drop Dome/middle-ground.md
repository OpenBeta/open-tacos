# Description
Off vertical face, slab and water courses. P1, 5.8 is a long excellent pitch. The rappel from the top of p1 to the top of the gulley is a 70 m rope stretcher. P2 is 5.6 or 5.7 and ends at the top of the Tear Drop.# Location
From p1 of Cougar's Patch walk left along the ledge and ascend gulley to it's top.# Protection
bolts, chain anchors, 70 meter tope.
