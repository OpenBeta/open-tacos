# Description
3 pitch route.  Fun easy climbing follows water courses, beautiful dykes and a fracture system for part of the last pitch.# Location
south west facing slab on left side of the tear drop formation.# Protection
p1 5 bolts. 65 ft.      p2 36 meters 10 bolts.    p3  35 meters 5 bolts.   p2 is the crux.   chain anchors on all pitches.  you need a 70 meter rope!
