# Description
Cool free standing pillar.  Crux is at the top.# Location
You will probably run into this tower on your approach to the "upper wall" of contra canyon towers.  It's at about the same elevation on the hill as the Upper Wall, and about 200 yards North.# Protection
3 bolts and chains at the top.
