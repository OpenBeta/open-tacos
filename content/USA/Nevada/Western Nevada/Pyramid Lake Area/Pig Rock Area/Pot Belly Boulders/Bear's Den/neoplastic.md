# Description
Great route that has seen very few repeats since it was FA'd in the '80s.Start on the undercling on the top of the roof with high feet. From a near horizontal start, launch to the tongue/tumor on the edge of the roof.Mantle and work your way up the face to the topout.# Location
Right in the middle of the rock. Can't miss the tongue of the rock. Pretty obvious line.# Protection
One pad. One spotter.
