# Description
Climb the S-shaped crack on the right side of the boulder face.  The route starts off with a left leaning hand crack and gradually widens and becomes very physical towards the top of the route.# Location
# Protection
Bring along hand-sized and larger gear.  Place your last #3 or 4, and fight for the top.
