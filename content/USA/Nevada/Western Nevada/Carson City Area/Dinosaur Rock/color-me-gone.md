# Description
Start right under Green Hell and traverse up and right to the prominent bulge/crack.  Bring 2" cam to protect before first bolt, otherwise run out with bad fall potential.  Climb up crack system, Clip third bolt, and traverse left.  These are some fun moves. Finish up on the Left Seam.# Location
Just to the right of Green Hell and The Left Seam.# Protection
8 bolts.
