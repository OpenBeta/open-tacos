# Description
Start on two large pockets to the right of Surfer's crack.  Move up to a single finger pocket with you left and crimps with your right. Pull over the small bulge on medium crimps and small juglets. Finish climbing pockets and jugs over the top of Surfer's crack.# Location
Two climbs to the right of Silver Surfer.  It begins right next to Surfer's crack, and ends in the same place as both climbs.# Protection
